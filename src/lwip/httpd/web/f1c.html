About F1C100S SoC

The Allwinner F1C100s is a small SoC in QFN88 package.

Main features:

- CPU: ARM ARM926EJ-S with 16KByte D-Cache and 32KByte I-Cache
- Memory: 32MB Embedded DDR (F1C100s) or 64MB Embedded DDR (F1C200s)
- Display: LCD RGB up to 1280x720 @ 60fps, TV CVBS (NTSC/PAL)
- Connectivity: USB OTG, SDIO, IR, I2S, 3 x TWI, 2 x SPI, 3 x UART
- Audio: integrated audio codec with stereo DAC (max 192kHz) and mono ADC (max 48kHz)
- Camera: 8-bit CMOS-sensor interface, CCIR656 for NTSC and PAL
- Video decoding 1280x720@30fps: H.264, MPEG1/2/4, MJPEG
- Video encoding: MJPEG 1280x720@30fps, JPEG up to 8192x8192
